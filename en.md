# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 25, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how Monokura ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information during its use by the developer ("we", "us", or "our").

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information

You can create an account using one of the following methods:

- **Google Account Login**: Display name, email address
- **Apple Account Login**: Display name, email address (may use Apple's "Hide My Email" relay address)
- **Email/Password Registration**: Display name, email address

This information is used for authentication purposes through Firebase Authentication.

### 1.2 User-Input Data

The following data is stored when using the App's features:

- Board names, location names
- Shopping list and inventory item information (name, quantity, unit, category, notes)
- Consumption history

This data is stored in Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore) and shared among members of the same board.

### 1.3 Advertising Information

The App uses Google AdMob to deliver advertisements. AdMob may collect the following information:

- Device identifiers (Ad ID)
- IP address
- Device information (OS version, device model, etc.)
- Information regarding ad views and clicks

On iOS 14.5 and later, we display a dialog requesting tracking permission via the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. If a user denies tracking, personalized ads will not be delivered.

### 1.4 Automatically Collected Information

The App may automatically obtain the following technical information through Firebase:

- Device type, OS version
- App version
- Crash logs (to improve App stability)

---

## 2. Purpose of Information Use

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- Ensuring account authentication and security
- Providing shopping list and inventory management features
- Data sharing among board members
- Delivering advertisements
- Improving the App and fixing bugs
- Responding to user support inquiries

---

## 3. Information Sharing and Third-Party Provision

We do not provide users' personal information to third parties except in the following cases:

- **Sharing with Board Members**: Members participating in the same board can view item data and display names within that board.
- **Service Providers**: The App uses the following third-party services. Please also refer to each service's privacy policy.
  - [Google Firebase](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) (Authentication, Database)
  - [Google AdMob](https://policies.google.com/privacy) (Ad delivery)
- **Legal Requests**: When there is a request for disclosure based on laws and regulations.

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- User data is stored on Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore) servers.
- Through Firebase security rules, users can only access their own data and data from boards they are participating in.
- All data transmission is encrypted (TLS).

---

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

- **Account Deletion**: You can delete your account from the settings screen. Upon account deletion, the user profile, owned boards, and all associated data will be deleted.
- **Consumption History**: Records of consumed items are automatically deleted after 7 days.
- **Guest Mode**: When used in guest mode, data is stored only in the device's local storage and is not sent to the server. Local data is automatically cleared upon login.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13.
